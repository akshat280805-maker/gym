# The 47-day shift

Atelier Athletic is a 240-member boutique studio in Indiranagar. Beautiful space, devoted community, and the same problem every operator has: a slow, invisible leak.

When Priya Raghavan deployed **GymnexAI**, the system spent its first week quietly listening — attendance cadence, payment health, WhatsApp engagement, trial follow-through.

By day eight, it had flagged 38 members at risk of churn and 26 dormant trials that had never been re-engaged.

## The compounding result

By day 47, retention had improved 31% and the previously dormant trial pipeline had converted **₹4.8L in new monthly revenue** — without Priya hiring a single new team member.

This is the operating layer the modern fitness business has been waiting for.
