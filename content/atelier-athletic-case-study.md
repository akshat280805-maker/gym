*ZESTO FITNESS STUDIO · IDAHO, UNITED STATES*

# How Zesto Fitness Studio added $4,000 in monthly revenue with GymnexAI

By identifying retention opportunities earlier, improving lead follow-up and giving the owner AI-powered operational visibility, Zesto Fitness Studio reported $4,000 in additional monthly revenue during its use of GymnexAI.

## Members and leads needed attention before it was too late.

Like many smaller fitness studios, Zesto needed a better way to see which members were losing momentum and which enquiries needed follow-up. Without a single operating view, the owner had to depend on manual updates and repeatedly check different parts of the business.

The opportunity was not to add more work. It was to make the right actions visible earlier.

## A clearer operating system for the owner.

### Retention Intelligence

GymnexAI helped surface members who needed attention earlier, allowing the studio to act before disengagement became a lost membership.

### Lead Follow-Up

Lead follow-ups became easier to organise and act on, helping the team stay responsive when prospective members showed interest.

### Personalised Member Experience

GymnexAI supported more personalised fitness engagement, helping members feel guided rather than left on their own.

### Nex Intelligence

The owner could use the in-app AI assistant to ask for gym insights, surface priorities and understand what needed action without manually reviewing every part of the operating system.

## Every morning, the owner knew where to look first.

GymnexAI generated a daily AI Morning Briefing designed to summarise important signals across the studio: members needing attention, lead follow-up priorities and operational insights. Instead of opening every section of the system, the owner could begin the day with one clear briefing.

## $4,000 in additional monthly revenue reported.

Through earlier retention action, more organised lead follow-up and clearer daily visibility, Zesto Fitness Studio reported $4,000 in additional monthly revenue while using GymnexAI.
