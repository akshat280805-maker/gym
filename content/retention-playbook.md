# Retention is a signal problem, not a willpower problem

The members you lose this quarter were already drifting last quarter. The signal was there. Nobody was watching.

GymnexAI watches six signals continuously:

1. Attendance cadence decay
2. Class booking abandonment
3. Engagement slowdown in WhatsApp
4. Billing health & card decline patterns
5. Trainer feedback flags
6. Recovery & wellness data

Each one triggers a respectful, personalised intervention — long before the member ever decides to leave.
